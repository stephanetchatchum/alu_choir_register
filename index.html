The Rules

ALU Choir
Constitution

Member responsibilities

  • Attend rehearsals, meetings and performances punctually — a 15-minute tolerance after the start time is allowed.
  • Actively take part in all choir activities, including rehearsals, sectional practice and performances.
  • Notify the Discipline Officer or Leadership Team in advance when unable to attend.
  • Prepare vocal parts and practise assigned music before rehearsals.
  • Treat all members, staff and volunteers with respect, cooperation and professionalism.
  • Follow the choir's dress code for performances and official events.
  • Meet all agreed financial obligations — registration fee, contributions, uniforms.

Disciplinary measures

Non-compliance may lead, in order of severity, to:

  • Verbal or written warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent dismissal from the choir
!
Specific sanctions may include re-doing probation or re-registration. Sanctions apply only to Official Members. The Leadership Team, guided by the Music Director, oversees discipline fairly and consistently.

Thresholds the app tracks

80%
Minimum attendance (excused absences included) to qualify for a pre-performance audition.
4×
Consecutive rehearsals a new member must attend — with the 5,000 RWF fee paid — to become an Official Member.
15'
Grace period after start time. Arriving later counts as Late.

Discipline Officer: records participation, ensures members follow the rules and constitution, keeps order, and addresses misconduct.
